1. Interface Feedback:
 
The interface feels slightly cluttered at the moment.
The green “New Service Available” notification and the holiday notification
seem a bit out of place in their current locations.

We also noticed that some of the information on the site appears to 
be placeholder or randomly generated content. While we understand this 
is likely temporary, we are still missing several pieces of accurate
information that need to be included.
 
2. Form Adjustments:
 
The “Community Resources” section displayed on the right side of the
intake form has proven to be a bit confusing for users, especially when
they're completing a long form.

We’d prefer to remove the “Community Resources” section from the intake 
form altogether and keep that content only in the “Resources” tab
